# 🚀 NiceSettings - Universal Roblox Optimization Pack
By Loto-dev1

This package optimizes your Roblox engine using FastFlags for maximum performance, FPS, and ultimate visibility across **all Roblox games**. Get the competitive edge for every fight!

---

### ✨ Features (v1.1 - Gambo Update):
* **Gambo Look:** No more dark corners! Dynamic shadows are completely disabled for an extremely bright, flat, and clean look.
* **Full Brightness & Clear Sky:** Forced zenith lighting adjustments so you maintain maximum visibility even during in-game night cycles.
* **Massive FPS Boost:** Disables heavy shaders to give you maximum frames per second (FPS) and smooth aiming, even on low-end PCs.
* **No Lag / Low Latency:** Optimized engine settings for the perfect peek during combat.

### 🛠 Installation:
1. Download the latest `NiceSettings.zip` from the **[Releases](https://github.com/Loto-dev1/NiceSettings/releases)** section on the right.
2. Extract the content (`ClientAppSettings.json`) into your Roblox or Bloxstrap directory (inside the `ClientSettings` folder).
3. Relaunch Roblox, join your favorite game, and enjoy the clean look!
